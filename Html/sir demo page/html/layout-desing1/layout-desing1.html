Maharashtra destinations for budget-conscious travellers

Maharashtra, beyond the glitzy-glamorous Mumbai, is a pretty budget-friendly state. And luckily for us, a lot of these places are pretty to look at too. Some of them are in the hills and some, in the quiet coastal areas. When visiting these places on a budget, consider staying in budget-friendly accommodations like guesthouses, hostels, or government-run tourist lodges.

One can explore its many historical sites, parks, and vibrant street markets without spending much. Famous tourist attractions like the Shaniwar Wada, Aga Khan Palace, or the Pune Okayama Friendship Garden have minimal entry fees.
